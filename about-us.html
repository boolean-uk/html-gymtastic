About Us

Gymtastic was founded in 1903 in London by John Gymtastic. Back then it only only a shed really, the equipment available were just bricks and the only exercises one could do there was brick-laying. Wait was this really a gym and not just an excuse to get free labour?!

Nowadays, our gyms are decked out with the latest equipment. Don't you worry, brick-laying is still an option!
